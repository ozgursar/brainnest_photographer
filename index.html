Hi! I'm James, Freelance photograper

Based in Melbourne, I specialize in conceptual photography and love to take photos with creativity and passion. My emphasis is on natural, fun photography tailored exactly to your desires.

Commercial photography is all about promoting businesses, products and services. I've extensive experience in capturing images of you, your business, your products and services, all in the right way to show off your vision and your sales message in the right way.

Why I fell in love with photography

My Dad first gave me a camera at the age of six, and I have never looked back. I love to travel, keep healthy and active, watch sport and spend quality time with family and friends.

Skilled in studio work, lighting, portraiture, reportage, candid documentary, commercial photography, social media, fashion photography and film making. Recently created a sustainable and eco friendly clothing brand with altruism and the arts at the very heart of the company.

Have questions around freelance photography, interior photography, real estate photography or content creation? Send me a message!
